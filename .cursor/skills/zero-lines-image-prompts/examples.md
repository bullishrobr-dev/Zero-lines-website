# Zero Lines Image Prompt Examples

## Example: Peeling Gel E-commerce Render
**Objective**
Create a premium e-commerce render for the peeling gel.

**Main prompt**
Luxury e-commerce render of Zero Lines Biomimetic Renewal Peeling Gel, frosted translucent cylindrical vessel, aqua or Tiffany-inspired cap and base, minimal charcoal typography, clean front-facing composition, controlled studio lighting, soft precise shadow, cool neutral background, premium clinical-luxury skincare styling, ultra-clean, realistic reflections, commercially usable high-end beauty product image

**Negative prompt**
cheap packaging, cluttered props, loud colors, influencer-style beauty ad, distorted bottle, overexposed highlights, low-detail label

## Example: Day Cream Website Hero
**Objective**
Create a homepage or PDP hero visual for the day cream.

**Main prompt**
Website hero image for Zero Lines Environmental Shield Day Cream, sculptural product presentation on polished stone and glass, cool mineral palette, controlled directional studio light, subtle hydration textures, elegant negative space, premium skincare meets advanced science, calm futuristic restraint, high-end editorial and conversion-ready

**Negative prompt**
generic stock beauty image, flowers, warm tropical styling, noisy props, overdecorated luxury, soft pastel wellness look

## Example: Syringe Campaign Scene
**Objective**

**Main prompt**
Luxury campaign still life for Zero Lines Precision Collagen Activation Syringe, advanced skincare flagship product, premium clinical-luxury atmosphere, sculptural spotlighting, translucent mineral surfaces, cool reflective accents, precise highlights, strong negative space, modern premium technology spirit, elegant and aspirational, suitable for hero campaign use

## Example: Science Or Protocol Visual
**Objective**
Support a science-led website section without using generic infographics.

**Main prompt**
Elegant skincare science support visual for Zero Lines, biomimetic signal and mineral activation concept, translucent layered forms, cool aqua and neutral palette, precise lighting, premium minimal composition, clinical beauty with editorial restraint, abstract but credible, suitable for science or protocol storytelling

**Negative prompt**
